United We Will Ride

Come One, Come All!

Welcome to Open Road Fanatics MCC

Come join a group full of motorcycle enthusiasts who love to get on the open road and ride. We do weekly rides and event rides like charity rides, etc. Come as you are! Weekly rides are usually followed with a post-ride meal.

All bikes welcomed! We are a free club who just like to ride and give back to the community.

Reach out and join if you are interested!

Yearly Events

Check the events tab regularly for any added events throughout the year

  1. Weekly Ride: Every Wednesday at 6pm. Meetup at Sugarland park by 5:50pm. Post-ride meal to follow. Location of post-ride meal will be determined the week of. More about the weekly rides can be found in the weekly rides tab.


  2. Monthly Meeting: Each monthly meeting will be held at Active gymnasium. Food will be ordered. You can play Basketball or Pickleball in the gym. Bring your family. We are a family-orientated club.


  3. Annual Charity Event: We will participate in the Toys for Tots charity ride October 28th. Bring either toys or cash with you to participate in the ride. A total of $30 required to participate in the ride if you choose the cash route. Kickstands up at 11am and meetup at Walmart by 10:45am to get registered.


  4. Annual Poker Run: We will host an annual Poker Run June 16th. We will meet up at Sally's bar. Be there by 10:45am to get registered for the ride. Kickstands up at 11am. Meet at Valley Station local hall after the run for food and prizes.


  5. Some of us ride up to Motorcycle Frenzy for their bike night each month. It is a great way to meet new people who have the passion of riding like us. Feel free to join if you like. The bike night is hosted every third Friday of the month. Food is provided. Come and hang out!
